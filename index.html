What is Vole?
Vole is an encoded language. The instructions are encoded by using total 16-bits that is also represented by hexadecimal digits. Instructions in vole machine consist of op-code and operation field of the register. First 4 bits are called the op-code and operation field has last 12 bits.
You can find the list of op-code meaning at here.
Why did I make this website?
Vole is hard to read and analyse. Serveral universities opt this language to explain the execution process of machine language and give students practices. Each questions are time-consuming to figure out the answers. Therefore I made this website to provide convenience.
